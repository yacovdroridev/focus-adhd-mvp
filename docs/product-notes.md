# Product notes

## Positioning

Focus Field is not a music generator for entertainment. It is a focus-state audio generator.

The main design rule: enough movement to keep the ADHD brain engaged, not enough novelty to steal attention.

## Current controls

- Mode: selects a base sound profile.
- Session length: sets timer duration.
- ADHD dial: increases/decreases stimulation.
- Volume: master output level.
- Noise bed: adds brown/pink-ish background texture.
- Pulse: adds rhythmic structure.
- Music bits: adds soft arpeggio/pluck notes so the result feels more musical and less like plain noise.
- Music bits amount: controls note presence/density.
- Melody pattern: selects a recognizable repeating motif.
- Melody clarity: controls how forward and defined the motif is.
- Song feel: adds proper musical structure with Tone.js instruments, chord progression, bassline, groove, effects, and repeated phrases.
- Groove: controls the strength of the beat/bass arrangement.
- Binaural drift: adds subtle left/right frequency drift.

## Design constraints

Avoid:

- Vocals
- Clear lead melodies
- Sharp transitions
- Big drops
- Sudden percussion
- Complex chord changes

Prefer:

- Slow modulation
- Stable tonality
- Gentle pulse
- Simple pentatonic/minor note patterns
- Short recognizable motifs
- Chord progressions and bassline movement
- Predictable texture
- Low-frequency warmth
